Atlanta Botanical Garden

Advice For Visitors:

  • Visit as early in the day as possible, at 9AM.
  • Get an annual pass, if you plan to visit more than once!
  • If you're only going to go ABG once, go in autumn!

I have visited the Atlanta Botanical Garden a few times. The last time I went was in October 2021, with my mother. The weather was perfect and I got to enjoy an expansive Halloween-themed section and a glass flower exhibition, which spanned the entire garden. I must have taken a hundred photos or so!
